The primary goal of this agent is to quickly and accurately retrieve information from a structured knowledge source. Instead of having employees manually search a large PDF or document, they can simply ask a question in natural language (e.g., "How many days of sick leave do I get?"), and the agent provides a concise, sourced answer.Core Problem SolvedEmployees often need quick answers to specific policy questions (e.g., leave, dress code, expense limits). Searching manually is tedious.
